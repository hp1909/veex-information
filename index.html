Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal financial data. This privacy policy explains how Veex handles your information.

Local Data Storage: Veex stores all your expense data locally on your device. Your financial information never leaves your phone unless you choose to backup to iCloud.

iCloud Backup: If you enable iCloud backup, your data is encrypted end-to-end and stored securely in your personal iCloud account.

No Data Collection: We do not collect, analyze, or share your personal expense data. Your financial information remains completely private.

Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app, but this never includes your actual expense data or personal information.
